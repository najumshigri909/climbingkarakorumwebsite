2. Booking Requirements

To complete your booking, we require:

  • A clear copy of your passport (photo page)
  • One recent passport-sized photograph
  • Completed booking form signed by each participant
  • Completed official application forms for permit processing

These documents should be emailed to our office at documents@climbingkarakorum.com

3. Permit Processing

Participants must submit all required documents at least 6 weeks before the trip start date. The permit approval process with the Gilgit-Baltistan Council typically takes 4-5 weeks.

Important: Once permits are issued, no refunds can be processed by the Gilgit-Baltistan Council.

4. Our Mission

Climbing Karakorum is committed to:

  • Ensuring fair wages and good working conditions for our staff
  • Supporting local communities through social projects
  • Promoting environmental sustainability and cultural respect

5. Our Responsibilities

We commit to:

  • Providing safe working conditions for all staff and porters

  • Supporting education initiatives for underprivileged communities
  • Delivering high-quality services to our clients

Note: We cannot be held responsible for itinerary changes due to factors beyond our control including weather, natural disasters, transportation delays, or political unrest.

6. Payment Policy

  • A 20% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm your booking
  • The remaining balance must be paid upon arrival in Pakistan
  • The deposit covers initial trip preparations and documentation

7. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made in writing. Refunds will be processed as follows:

Cancellation Period Refund Amount
More than 60 days before departure 70% refund (minus bank charges)
30-60 days before departure 60% refund (minus bank charges)
15-30 days before departure 50% refund (minus bank charges)
10-15 days before departure 30% refund (minus bank charges)
Less than 10 days before departure No refund

No refunds will be given if you leave during the trip for any reason, including health issues or external circumstances.

9. Travel Insurance

All participants must obtain comprehensive travel insurance covering:

  • Trip cancellation
  • Medical expenses and emergency evacuation
  • Lost or damaged baggage
  • Personal liability

Proof of insurance must be provided at least 30 days before departure.

10. Privacy Policy

We collect personal information solely for trip organization purposes. Your data will:

  • Never be shared with third parties without consent
  • Only be used for your booking and necessary permits

11. Complaints

If you have any concerns during your trip:

  1. Inform your guide immediately to allow resolution

We will investigate all complaints and respond within 30 days.

Participants must:

  • Maintain appropriate behavior

16. Travel Disruptions

Mountain travel may be affected by:
