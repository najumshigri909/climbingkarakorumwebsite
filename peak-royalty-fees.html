Mountain Climbing Royalty Fees

Pakistan's Gilgit-Baltistan Region

Climbing Fee Regulations

Small Peaks Expadetion

No fees apply for mountains below 6,500 meters in elevation.

Regional Discount

Selected districts (Chitral, Gilgit, Ghizer) enjoy a 10% fee reduction, excluding Spantik/Golden Peak.

Winter Climbing Incentive

All peaks have a reduced 5% fee during December through February.

General Discount

40% discount available for most peaks, with some exceptions.

Mountain Climbing Royalty Fees

Pakistan's Gilgit-Baltistan Region

Climbing Fee Regulations

Seasonal Variations

Royalty fees vary by season (Summer, Autumn, Winter) with different rates for each period.

Nationality Based

Different fee structures apply for foreigners and Pakistani nationals.

Height Categories

Fees are structured according to mountain height categories from 6500m up to K2 (8611m).

Trekking Fees

Separate trekking fees apply for general trekking activities.
